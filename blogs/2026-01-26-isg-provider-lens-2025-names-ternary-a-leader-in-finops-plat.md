<p><a href="https://isg-one.com/">Information Services Group (ISG)</a>, a global technology research and advisory firm, has released its 2025 ISG Provider Lens<sup>®</sup> for Multi Public Cloud Solutions, FinOps Platforms. The report evaluates 24 leading FinOps platforms, assessing their product capabilities, market strength, and ability to support modern multi-cloud environments.</p>



<p>In this blog, we’ll briefly explain what the ISG Provider Lens is, why it matters, and what Ternary’s 2025 Leader recognition means for organizations evaluating FinOps platforms.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-understanding-the-isg-provider-lens-quadrants">Understanding the ISG Provider Lens quadrants</h2>



<p>The ISG Provider Lens positions vendors across four quadrants based on portfolio attractiveness and competitive strength:</p>


<div class="wp-block-list-wrapper">
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li><strong>Leaders</strong>. Platforms with strong capabilities and broad enterprise adoption, designed for complex multi-cloud environments.</li>



<li><strong>Product Challengers. </strong>Solutions with deep and differentiated functionality that continue to invest heavily in FinOps innovation.</li>



<li><strong>Market Challengers. </strong>Vendors with solid market traction and visibility, offering reliable FinOps capabilities.</li>



<li><strong>Contenders. </strong>Emerging or focused solutions addressing specific FinOps needs or earlier-stage requirements.</li>
</ul>
</div>


<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-how-ternary-compares">How Ternary compares</h2>



<p>Ternary was named one of six Leaders in the 2025 ISG Provider Lens<sup>®</sup>, marking the <a href="https://ternary.app/blog/isg-multicloud-finops-leader-2024/">second consecutive year</a> of recognition as a leading FinOps platform.</p>



<p>According to Shashank Rajmane, Principal Analyst at ISG:</p>



<blockquote class="wp-block-quote is-layout-flow wp-block-quote-is-layout-flow">
<p>Ternary&#8217;s solution is specifically designed as a multicloud FinOps platform that supports all major hyperscalers, offering a single source of truth based standardized reporting and deep integration, allowing for workload specific optimization.</p>
</blockquote>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-why-this-recognition-matters">Why this recognition matters</h2>



<p>FinOps platforms are more than tools. They have become foundational infrastructure for how organizations govern cloud and technology spend.</p>



<p>Recognition from firms like ISG signals that a platform:</p>


<div class="wp-block-list-wrapper">
<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Has been evaluated against real enterprise requirements</li>



<li>Demonstrates technical and operational maturity</li>



<li>Continues to innovate in response to evolving FinOps needs</li>
</ul>
</div>


<p>For organizations navigating rapid cloud growth and increasing financial scrutiny, this independent validation provides critical confidence in platform selection.</p>
